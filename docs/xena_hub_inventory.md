\# UCSC Xena Hub Inventory



\## Purpose



`core/search/xena\_hub\_inventory.py` creates a curated inventory of UCSC Xena public data hubs relevant to cancer multi-omics atlas construction.



This module is part of Milestone 1E.2: first external-source expansion beyond GDC.



\## Why UCSC Xena?



UCSC Xena provides public data hubs with ready-to-use matrices for cancer and biomedical omics resources, including TCGA, GDC, ICGC, Pan-Cancer Atlas, PCAWG, Toil RNA-seq, Treehouse, and other public resources.
